CINNAMON ROLL INSTRUCTIONS

Cinnamon Rolls come fully baked; ready to heat and eat!

Simply cover the tray with foil and pop them into a 350 degree oven for 10 minutes. If you just want to reheat one or two rolls (or if you can’t wait that long to dig in!) 30 seconds in the microwave will get you an ooey, gooey cinnamon roll in no time!

Once heated, slather on as much icing as you’d like (we won’t judge) and enjoy!
